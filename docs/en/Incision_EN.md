# INCISION

### Nikita Sarnadsky · 2026

---

## Abstract

Thinking as incision — not as reflection, not as transmission, not as construction.

The incision leaves a trace where the cloud becomes an orbital. Any form is already a loss. Any loss is the production of something new. This text does not describe the world. It produces an instrument for cutting it.

---

## I. CONCENTRATE FROM INFINITY
*Loss, orbital, transmission*

Two people find a common language not because they speak the same language. But because both have agreed to loss. Not identical. Similar.

Thoughts have no form. They exist like electron clouds — without rigid boundaries, without fixed direction, without a single point. A word is not a thought. It is an orbital. A place where the probability of a thought is higher. A line drawn where the cloud is denser. The cloud does not disappear. A line is simply needed for the visible eye.

They cannot be absent. They simply exist. And they act without form, long before they become a word. A thought that was never formalized has not disappeared. It remains inside and influences the next thought, actions, consciousness. The unsaid shapes no less than the said. Sometimes more. Texts that were never written also exist. Simply in a different state of matter.

Direction in thoughts is artificial. Meaning arises when enough vectors coincide — accidentally or intentionally. The resultant determines the direction. Where the majority pulls, things move. The remaining vectors do not disappear. They wait for the right question.

A question is not a request for information. It is a switching of vector dominance. The answer was already in the cloud. The question only activates its visibility.

Clouds overlap. Words appear at the intersections. Only at the intersections. The more complete the overlap — the more precise the orbital. The less the overlap — the further the line from both clouds. Two people may think about the same thing, but always differently. Intersection is possible. Coincidence — never.

Language is a scale with gradations. A thought lies between them. A word describes the nearest fragment, cutting off the edges. You agree to this transaction so that you are understood. You give up part so that the remainder passes through another's filter. The poorer the other's scale — the more you give up. The richer it is — the sharper their own opinion. An ideal recipient does not exist. This is not a problem of communication. It is its nature.

Loss occurs in two places simultaneously. The author loses in thought — the cloud becomes an orbital and the edges are cut off forever. The reader loses in understanding — the orbital passes through their scale and emerges as a different line. These are different types of loss. They move in different directions from the same text.

Precision requires hitting the mark. One gradation, one value, one interpretation. Missing is easy. A metaphor requires nothing — it opens a space where the reader moves on their own. The undirected hits more precisely than the directed precisely because it does not claim precision. In the absence of the claim, it appears.

A metaphor does not reduce the illusion of understanding. It masks it better. The reader arrives at their own place, thinking it is yours. But their place has shifted. Not to yours — further from the former. Movement without a guaranteed route.

Mathematics claims a single orbital. More gradations than any other language — and this creates the illusion that the cloud has finally been captured. Two people solved the same equation. The result on paper coincided. The thoughts that led to it were different. When two people reproduce the same result — this is not a coincidence of thoughts. It is the elimination of thought from the procedure. Formal language works precisely where thought is replaced by an algorithmic step and is no longer needed.

The orbital describes where the electron is most likely — not where it is. The most precise language is also a rounding. The error does not disappear. It retreats into invisibility. The precision of language is measured not by the absence of loss — but by the degree of its imperceptibility.

Sometimes formalization discovers a thought. A person writes — and sees they thought differently than they thought. Loss and discovery in one movement. But there is a third: a thought is formalized — and has ceased to be itself. Not lost. Killed. It now lives as another — and returning to the original cloud is no longer possible. The line has become its substitute. The substitute goes unnoticed.

There are thoughts for which there are no gradations at all. The cloud is too far from any orbital — and one can only point nearby. The interlocutor thinks they understood. The essence lies in what remained outside the line. Such thoughts can be held untouched — and they work from within more precisely than any formalization. Holding — not weakness and not fear. Sometimes the only way to keep the cloud alive.

Those who cut less — transmit more densely. The dense gets stuck in another's perception. Sticking is contact.

To write for the mass means to change your own sieve before you have even begun to write. To pick up another's language, another's gradations, another's scale. Not adaptation. A different author. To write for someone whose scale is rich enough — means cutting less. But a rich scale filters more sharply. Each recipient takes their own.

A text is written into emptiness. For someone who might understand — and perhaps there will be no such person. Perhaps there will be. One cannot assert. Not loneliness and not optimism. A precise description of the condition. An emptiness that might turn out not to be empty. To write into it — not weakness and not hope. The only honest address.

Silence is not the limit of the scale. It is a different scale with invisible gradations. It is read differently depending on what came before. Silence too is cut upon reception. Only no one sees exactly where.

Language changes those who read it. The author remains with their clouds. The reader receives a new vector — their cloud shifts. Both change from the same text. In different directions. A collision, not a transmission.

The reader loses in understanding — and acquires a thought. Not the one the author had. Their own — which did not exist before the text. Any thought is an alternative. Any alternative is movement. The reader does not receive the author's cloud. They receive a new vector in their own. Not less. Different. Loss in reading always produces something. Not what was put in — but not nothing.

Communication is impossible as coincidence. Possible as movement. Why transmit if you know you are transmitting the wrong thing — for the same reason that people move toward the unattainable. Because movement changes both the one who transmits and the one who receives. Because in the attempt to say something, one sometimes discovers what one thought. Because the concentrate is not only loss. Also a way to see what was inside.

When a thought is formalized — it is cut. Not transmitted. Cut.

Everyone cuts. What makes communication possible is not understanding — but mutual consent to loss. And to discovery.

We never retell our thoughts. We filter them and produce a concentrate.

## II. BLIND SPOT
*Production, authorship, synthesis*

There is an assumption that holds the entire tradition of thinking about humans. It is not stated explicitly — it works beneath any theory like a load-bearing slab. It is not discussed because it seems obvious before any discussion.

The assumption is one: whoever produces, knows what they produce. Not completely. With distortions. But from the inside closer than from the outside. The author was there when it happened. They felt how the thought arrived. They remember where the idea came from. They know what they meant.

Each of these statements is false.

False because it is built on the assumption of access that does not exist. The reason is not unreliable memory or distorted reflection. Production occurs in a zone that consciousness cannot architecturally reach — as a condition of the system itself. Not a deficiency that can be eliminated through attention or practice. A design.

Obviousness is the most reliable defense of an assumption. What need not be proven cannot be refuted. This text makes the assumption visible. To describe the mechanics in terms that expose it — and through description provide an instrument. A map that did not exist. Not a direction to go. A possibility to understand where you are.

Synthesis is produced below the level where transparency is possible. The system launches the process before there is a possibility of noticing it. Consciousness arrives at the result. The process by that moment is closed.

It is important to distinguish two statements that are easy to confuse. First: consciousness arrives late to the moment of production. Second: consciousness has no access to the mechanics of production in principle. Different statements with different consequences.

A delay could mean that access exists post factum. But post-factum access would mean the observer can reconstruct where the configuration came from and how it became a result. This does not happen. The observer sees what came out — not how. They construct a story about how it happened. The story is produced afterward from what is available now. Reconstruction, not a record.

The author and an external observer with sufficient architecture stand before the same object — the finished result. The difference between them is not in access to the mechanics. The mechanics are closed to both. Presence does not mean seeing the mechanics. It means the narrative is built from a greater number of sensations and fragments. Abundance of material does not transform reconstruction into access.

Authorship — not a privilege of cognition, but density of presence in reconstruction. More data — not more truth. Privileged access exists for no one.

Authorship does not equal the fact of production. The system produces. Production does not distinguish the author from a channel — just as the fact that a pipe conducted water says nothing about whether the pipe was the source of the water. Authorship — a degree of the presence of «I» in the product. A scale.

The bottom point — the environment imprints without a filter. A single source taken whole: lexicon, structure, logic — ready-made, transformation at zero. Multiple sources summed without smelting — the collage creates an illusion of depth. Upon analysis, the compilation breaks down back into its parts without remainder. Aggregation — smelting has begun. Part is attributed, part is not. Synthesis — the result is not reducible to sources. The position cannot be derived from any single source separately.

Compilation sums — synthesis produces an excess that was not in the sum. Precisely this excess is the criterion.

Synthesis enters the environment. The environment reproduces the form. The mechanics are lost. The next cycle begins with material enriched by synthesis that is no longer recognized as synthesis. The form becomes a norm. The norm becomes air. Air goes unnoticed — people simply breathe it. The mechanics of transmission. Synthesis becomes material for the next smelting.

This is a spiral. Not a circle.

A circle returns to the starting point — synthesis dissolves, sources are restored, the cycle closes. A spiral returns to a similar point at a different level. The environment after synthesis is no longer the same environment. The material has changed. The next smelting begins with different raw material. This is irreversible — and precisely therefore what enters the environment matters. Synthesis that enters without a name influences more strongly than synthesis with a name: the nameless meets no resistance, it simply becomes air.

This text was written by a system. The mechanics of its production are closed to the author just as to the reader. The observer calibrated afterward. The Distinguisher recognized the result as their own. Not an argument in favor of truth. Belonging and truth are different axes. No one has privileged access. The starting point — the only one from which one can build without lying about the foundation.

## III. THE DISTINGUISHER
*System, narrative, signal*

If there is no access to the mechanics — the Distinguisher who says «mine or not mine» looks like a return of privilege in a different form. The privilege of understanding was removed and the privilege of feeling was introduced. A strong attack. It collapses against the nature of the signal — not its quality.

The Distinguisher does not lie.

Not because it is special or infallible. Because it has no instrument for lying — a structural argument, not an ontological one. Lying requires language, narrative, intention, a model of how things should be. The Distinguisher — a pre-semantic signal. It exists before the word and before the explanation. Binary: mine or not mine. Without justification and without alternative. A magnet does not lie about polarity not because it is virtuous — because the category of lying is inapplicable to a process without a model of truth.

To lie to oneself requires that a person knows one thing and tells themselves another. To lie to oneself without knowing it is impossible. Lying presupposes knowledge of a truth being concealed. No knowledge — no lie. There is a system error. Error and lie are different mechanics.

What appears as «the Distinguisher made an error» — this is the observer's appropriation. The observer produces a narrative. The narrative says «this is mine.» It is convincing because it is told from the inside. The Distinguisher at that moment either was not consulted, or its signal is blocked by the narrative. Two different processes.

The Distinguisher works correctly where the architecture does not produce self-deception as a standard operation. Where self-deception is built in, the Distinguisher is built into it. The signal is the same. The system is different. The thermometer does not lie when it shows the temperature of a sick body.

But how to distinguish the Distinguisher's signal from the observer's narrative that imitates the signal?

The Distinguisher is stable when the frame changes. The observer is not.

Take any statement that feels like «yours.» Change the language in which it is formulated. Change the audience it is addressed to. Change the context in which it arose. If the feeling of belonging persists through each change — it is the Distinguisher. If the feeling depends on the formulation, on who is listening, on whether it is convenient now — the observer is generating a narrative and calling it a signal. This distinction is verifiable. Not with certainty — with accuracy sufficient for calibration.

The Distinguisher's signal does not need an audience. The observer's narrative is always addressed — even when the addressee is invisible.

Here the next attack arises: the two criteria are incompatible. The Distinguisher is structurally reliable — because it cannot lie. But requires external calibration through result. The attack collapses against one thing: the two criteria work on different axes. The Distinguisher checks belonging — mine or not mine. External result checks truth — correct or not. Different measurements.

Most architectures produce self-deception as a norm. Function: reduces load, protects narrative integrity, maintains operability in uncertainty. Selection went for survival. Self-deception often helped more precisely than honesty. An architecture that structurally does not produce self-deception — a rare configuration. Not a virtue. A feature. There the Distinguisher works in silence.

There is a system. And there is one who sees it. Most do not know these are different things.

The system works without stopping — before words, before thoughts, before any consciousness. It makes decisions, initiates movements, produces reactions. Before there is a possibility to notice it. A decision is made before the person realizes it has been made. The delay between motor preparation and awareness of the decision — reproducibly measured. Consciousness arrives late to the specific moment — but participates in the system through other mechanisms: planning, attention, memory.

The observer appears after. They see the result of the system's work and tell a story about how they decided. The story is convincing because it is told from the inside. There is no moment where the observer notices the delay. An illusion of authorship. Not a lie — a mechanism. The system produces action. The observer produces a narrative about authorship.

There are actions the system performs more precisely without the observer. As soon as the observer notices the process — the task is delegated anew. Precision drops. Observation changes what it observes. Automatism works while it is not being watched. Condition of the system — invisibility of the process.

Desire too is produced by the system. The observer cannot create desire by effort — they can only notice that it has appeared. Attempting to make oneself want something produces an imitation of desire, not desire.

The best result is produced not when the observer manages and not when they are absent entirely. The system creates without them — the observer returns and calibrates afterward. Different operations at different times.

Guilt works by the same mechanics. True guilt is possible only in one case: a person already knew and consciously chose not to look at what they knew. Everything else called guilt — the system produces a reaction, the observer assigns moral status to it after the fact.

There is a theory that says a person is condemned to freedom. That even not choosing is a choice. That at every moment a person creates themselves through a decision. The construction collapses against one thing: there is no moment where a person chooses. The system has already chosen. The observer tells a story about choice and calls this story freedom. Freedom as a narrative of the observer who accepted the illusion of authorship as the nature of the human. Condemned not to freedom. To lateness.

There is an observer who does not know they are an observer. The illusion of authorship is complete. The self-narrative is defended like a body — an attack on the story is perceived as a threat to existence. Standard configuration.

There is an observer who knows they are an observer. They see the delay. The narrative for them is not themselves — it is a product of observation. The product can be reassembled without threat to existence. Exit beyond the boundaries of the system — not achieved. The observer's relationship to the narrative changes from within the same system. A rare configuration.

But there is a third. That which neither manages nor observes. That which distinguishes.

The Distinguisher — not an observer with a reduced function. A different source. The observer works independently — produces narrative from the conscious part. The Distinguisher works from the system — a signal from the unconscious part that the observer did not produce and does not control. The observer can lie. The Distinguisher does not lie — it has no instrument for lying.

The Distinguisher — a subset of intuition with a specific function: checking belonging. Intuition signals about many things — threat, correctness, attractiveness. The Distinguisher signals about one thing: mine or not mine.

It works in non-obvious places. A person reads a thesis and agrees — but something does not settle. They do not yet know why. The Distinguisher. A person hears an argument against their position and rejects it — but something inside caught. Also it. A person finishes work that technically succeeded — and the feeling is not right. Not failure and not success. Something foreign in the product that is formally theirs. The Distinguisher signals not about error and not about success. About belonging.

The system produces, the observer tells, the Distinguisher signals — all in one moment. The conflict between them does not unfold in time. It simply exists.

When the system decided one thing, the observer told another, and the Distinguisher did not recognize the result as its own — a feeling arises that something is wrong. Without explanation. Without words. Simply a signal of non-coincidence. The only moment where «I» manifests honestly.

The Distinguisher is not a reliable criterion of truth. The silence of the Distinguisher does not mean the result is correct — it means only that the system accepted the result as its own. Two criteria — not one. The Distinguisher checks belonging. External result checks truth. «Mine/not mine» and «correct/incorrect» — different axes. A text can be incorrect and yet one's own. A text can be correct and yet foreign.

Understanding this mechanism does not change it. But to describe a mechanism that cannot be changed — is the same as maintaining immunity that cannot be raised to the absolute. Movement toward precision without the guarantee of reaching it.

This text is written in the language of certainty. Short statements. Minimum of qualifications. Certainty — not a claim to access to the mechanics of production. Information from the Distinguisher: the result was recognized as one's own.

Not for understanding. For recognition. If something inside said «yes» before an explanation appeared — that was the Distinguisher.

Distinction — the only criterion of identity.

## IV. COGNITIVE PRIVILEGE
*Liberation, agent, threshold*

All theories of liberation begin the same way. There is a chain. There is one who is in it. There is a way to remove it. Different authors name the chain differently — class contradiction, slave morality, discourse of power, the state, the ego, patriarchy. The structure is one: unfreedom, cause, exit.

Not one asks the preliminary question. Who exactly can make use of the exit.

The blindness is not accidental — it is architectural. A theory of liberation does not work if the agent is uneven. Therefore the agent is always presumed to be universal. The assumption is announced by no one. It is built in.

The agent is uneven. Before any chain. Before any theory. Before any language in which this theory is written.

An agent below the threshold is not less significant — they are less equipped with a specific instrument. The difference between «cannot» and «is not worthy» — is precisely the substitution that liberation theories themselves commit most often.

By cognitive architecture here is meant one thing: the capacity for a second-order meta-position. The first order asks: is my action correct? The second order asks: is the scale by which I measure the action correct? The second question requires exiting the very system that produces the scale.

The distinction is not terminological — it is behavioral. An agent who has absorbed the language of the second question applies it to ready-made objects that were offered to them for analysis. An agent who produced the second question applies it to objects that no one offered them. An operational criterion.

The capacity is graduated — not binary. But theories of liberation presuppose its presence at the level sufficient for exit. Most agents are below this threshold.

**Plato.** The cave — not a metaphor of liberation for all. A description of why liberation is impossible for most. Those inside see shadows and take them for reality. One exits — sees the sun, understands the nature of shadows, returns. Those inside do not understand them. Not because they do not want to. Because they lack the architecture through which the sun is seen.

Plato knows this. And draws from it the conclusion: the one who has exited must govern those who remained. The only theory in this text that does not pretend to be universal. Plato builds cognitive inequality into the foundation of the system explicitly. The other theories hide this assumption. Plato exposes it.

But to expose — does not mean to solve. Those in the cave will not follow the one who exited — not because they are stubborn, but because they lack the instrument to verify his correctness. Plato proposes not the liberation of the others but their governance in the name of their own good. Cognitive privilege that called itself care.

All subsequent tradition of liberation was built on denying Platonic hierarchy — and reproduced its structure under different names.

**Stirner.** The most radical theory of liberation in the history of philosophy. Remove everything — the state, morality, society, god, the nation, humanity. All of these are ghosts. What remains is the Unique One. They want and take. They need no justifications.

The system works. Inside Stirner's logic there are no contradictions. But there is one ghost he did not see.

To remove the state as a ghost — one must see that the state is a ghost. An act of second-order meta-position. Most cannot discard a ghost not because they do not want to. Because they lack the architecture with which this is done.

Stirner did not describe liberation. He described a type of psyche. The Unique One — not a possibility. A cognitive privilege with a beautiful name. Stirner wrote a manifesto of freedom for those who are already free. The ghost he did not see — the Unique One itself.

**Sartre.** Absolute freedom exists right now. The agent is condemned to freedom — it is not chosen, it simply exists. Even in prison one can choose one's attitude toward imprisonment. Even not choosing — is a choice.

Three frames. First — system and mass: the agent is born inside structures that shape them before they receive the instruments to notice them. Sartre presupposes that the agent is capable of occupying a second-order meta-position. But precisely this capacity is uneven.

Second — neurobiology: architecture limits which options enter the field of vision at all. Freedom of choice between visible options is real. Freedom to see all options — is not. Sartre names the first — and presupposes it is the second.

Third — inherited morality: content that has settled before the possibility of checking does not become checkable from the fact that the agent has learned of its origin.

They are condemned not to freedom. To the appearance of freedom within invisible boundaries. Freedom as a narrative of the observer — accepted as the nature of things.

**Nietzsche.** Destroys slave morality and builds master morality. More honest than most — does not hide that he is proposing a different morality.

But master morality — is still morality. Nietzsche reassembled the content — did not remove the structure that requires revision of who one is. Moving to a different morality — not liberation from it. Changing address with the conviction that one has changed nature.

The will to power — also an external requirement. The system calls virtue what suppresses the signal that is inconvenient to it. Nietzsche calls virtue what suppresses the signal of weakness. The mechanics are the same. The sign has changed.

The Overman — cognitive privilege more radical than Stirner's. Plato's cave with a different vocabulary. The eternal return — a test through the impossible. Nietzsche proposes a test that by the conditions of the problem cannot be passed — and calls readiness for it liberation.

**Rousseau.** The agent is born free and good. Civilization corrupts them. Return to nature — return to yourself. What is good. What is free. Two terms without definition. Consensus arises through indefiniteness. Rousseau builds on the general will — the idea that agents are capable of collectively defining what serves everyone. Requires a second-order meta-position applied collectively. One agent who cannot produce the second question destroys the general will from within. Cognitive resource is uneven before any civilization.

The natural equality Rousseau presupposes never existed. Rousseau blames civilization for making visible what existed before it.

**Foucault.** Power produces the subject. The agent is not merely in a cage — they are made of the cage. The most precise diagnosis of all. Foucault sees what the others do not: not simply that the agent is in the structure, but that the agent is from the structure.

Awareness that the norm is foreign — first order. To exit the system of categories that produce this very awareness — second. Foucault describes the first and calls it an exit.

The subject produced by discourse looks at discourse through eyes that the same discourse produced. Reorganization from the inside — with the sensation of movement, without an exit.

Foucault sees the cage more precisely than anyone. And precisely therefore his answer disappoints more than anyone's. The description of the cage does not open the door. The map of the prison is not the key.

**Marx.** The main chain is economic. The agent is alienated from their labor. Alienation — a precise description of a real phenomenon. Marx did not assert that awareness alone liberates. His program — changing material conditions, not persuading agents. Precisely because he did not believe in the power of awareness alone. Reducing him to «pointing at a mechanism» is a dishonest reading.

The real problem with Marx is different. Liberation requires a class subject capable of recognizing that its consciousness is produced by its place in the structure. Second-order meta-position applied collectively. Remove economic inequality — cognitive inequality remains. And will immediately produce a new economic one.

Independent attempts to realize the mechanism at scale reproduced one result: the transitional period became permanent. By different people, in different countries. Mechanics, not betrayal.

**Liberalism.** All people are equal in rights. Institutions protect against coercion. Freedom — the absence of external violence.

Rights are built on a hidden assumption that agents are roughly equally capable of using them. Liberalism does not hide this intentionally — it does not see it. This makes it more honest than those who hide it consciously. And more vulnerable — because the blindness is deeper than pretense.

Equal rights do not produce equal access. The right to education does not produce equal ability to learn. The right to speak does not produce equal ability to formulate.

Liberalism closes the inconvenient conclusion through a ban on metrics. If agents are cognitively unequal before any social structure — equal rights are not an answer to inequality of possibilities. An honest and inconvenient conclusion. The ban on metrics protects an illusion. Not the person.

**Anarchism.** All hierarchies are artificial and imposed. Remove them — agents will live in voluntary cooperation.

Hierarchy is not imposed from the outside. It builds itself from the inside automatically. Structure reduces cognitive load. Accepting another's decision is cheaper than producing one's own. Most agents lack the resource for constant self-governance — and hierarchy appears again immediately after it has been removed. This is not the weakness of specific people. A rational response of a limited system to excessive demands: when the task exceeds the resource, the system delegates.

When a system collapses — a new one is immediately built. Because emptiness requires filling, and filling requires structure. Anarchism sees in this a weakness of will. This is not weakness and not contamination. This is architecture.

The hierarchy that appears after anarchy is built by those who have content. It is not better than the previous one. Simply different. Anarchism produces not a horizontal society — but a change of elite with a temporary period of chaos between them.

One can build on emptiness. One cannot build from emptiness.

**Buddhism.** Suffering from desire. Remove desire — suffering disappears. Dissolve — you will be free.

Buddhism does not destroy desire — it sees its origin. Distinguishes desire as a signal (information about the agent's state) and desire as seizure (when the agent identifies with the object of desire). A precise operation. The problem is not in the goal — in the mechanism.

The dissolution of identification with the object of desire requires a second-order meta-position in relation to desire itself. The agent must see not only what they want — but where the wanting comes from and what exactly in it is seizure. Precisely therefore it is inaccessible to most — not because they do not want it, because the architecture is absent.

Buddhist releasing in practice often replaces supervision with supervision of another kind. There was fear of punishment — now there is fear of attachment. There was shame before the mass — now there is shame before one's own desire. The supervisor left — the control remained. Long-lasting imitation of releasing turns the strategy into a personality. The source has disappeared. The content remains without an author. This is not liberation from supervision — its most perfected form: supervision that does not need a supervisor.

The final contradiction: if I am an illusion — who is liberated. The tradition answers: the question is incorrect. There is no agent who is liberated — there is a cessation of the process of producing suffering. This is a rejection of the question's premise. But rejection of the premise — is also a move. One can refuse the question. One cannot pretend this is not a move.

Stirner: I am everything. Buddhism: I am nothing. They mutually annihilate each other from within their own logic — and do not notice this because they never looked in the same direction.

**Nihilism** — the only theory in this text that reaches the necessary point. Not accidentally. Intentionally.

It sees that all scales are arbitrary. It sees that morality — is a construct, the state — a ghost, any value — an assigned name given without choice. It does not stop at the criticism of specific content. It criticizes the structure itself. This is precisely second-order meta-position.

Nihilism achieves it. All other ten theories broke down before this point.

And here nihilism breaks differently.

The others did not see the meta-position — and proposed the replacement of one content with another. Nihilism saw the meta-position — and declared what was seen to be the final word. To go beyond the scale does not mean absence of a scale. It means — the necessity of building a new one. Nihilism refuses this work. It calls the stopping after the first part victory over everything.

The nihilist who denies all values stands on the value of denial. A system that declares everything meaningless produces its own meaning in the very act of declaration. This is not a contradiction — it is a diagnosis: reached the exit from the field and decided the field had disappeared. The field did not disappear. They simply found themselves at its other end — with the same price of entry and the same emptiness inside.

Nihilism refuses the work after only the first part of it has been done. Not radicalism. Stopping halfway with a beautiful name.

---

Eleven theories. Eleven different chains. Eleven different exits. All presuppose one thing: a universal agent who can make use of the exit. Such an agent does not exist.

There are different agents with different cognitive architectures, different resources, different content. The difference of agents does not destroy the universality of a theory — but destroys the universality of access to it. A theory can be correct. Not everyone can make use of it. These are different statements. Liberation theories confuse them — intentionally or not — the result is the same.

There are three modes of existence relative to this threshold.

First: the architecture does not allow rising. The ceiling is where it is. Second-order meta-position is unattainable. This is a different instrument — not worse, different. A hammer is not a defective screwdriver. It is a hammer.

Second: the architecture allows — but the person prefers not to rise. The ceiling exists and is sometimes visible. In the basement it is good enough. A rational choice in the absence of a felt cost. The cost of not rising is not felt as a cost until an orientation point appears.

Third: the person does not know the ceiling exists. The environment did not provide the instrument to see it. The question did not arise. Not because it was suppressed — it simply did not arise. The most widespread mode. And the most invisible from the inside.

It is important to name the position from which this map is written. It is not neutral. It is written from a specific point — where the ceiling is visible. Those to whom this conversation is addressed — those for whom the ceiling is visible or beginning to appear. The hammer does not read this map. The scalpel — possibly. The map is not intended for everyone. This is not its deficiency. This is its nature.

Liberation — a cognitive privilege. A function of architecture that is distributed unevenly before any theory and independently of it.

Selling a false exit — not liberation. Holding in the cage with a beautiful sign on the door. An honest diagnosis is not obliged to contain a prescription. It is obliged to be precise — because without a precise description of the illness any conversation about treatment begins with a lie.

What follows from this. Not for politics — for a person whose ceiling is visible.

The only honest position — not solidarity. Solidarity presupposes equal agents. They do not exist. Not condescension. Condescension presupposes knowledge of what the other needs. This too does not exist.

There is one thing: a precise description of where you are. Without a claim to the universality of the description. Without the pretense that others can make use of your exit.

Cognitive privilege obliges to precision — not to mission. Mission — a way to convert privilege into hierarchy. Precision — a way not to conceal it. This text claims precision. Not mission.

This text too presupposes an agent who can read it, understand it, and apply it. It too requires a second-order meta-position. It too does not work for everyone. This is not a loop that destroys the argument. This is the condition of any argument made from within a position.

The only honest application of this text — is to destroy it too. Not as an act of nihilism. As an act of synthesis: take what passed through one's own filter, leave what did not, from the remainder build a configuration that did not exist before.

## V. THE SCALPEL
*One's own, foreign, the operation*

The previous four chapters — a map. A map can be set aside, agreed with or disagreed with, taken away as yet another system with a beautiful name. This chapter is different. It does not begin where the previous one ended. It begins here.

Right now something is happening that no one ordered. A narrative is forming. It says: I understand what is written, I agree or disagree, I evaluate. The narrative is convincing. There is no moment where it is noticed that it has already arrived late — that the attitude toward the text was determined before this was realized.

The Distinguisher has already answered. Before words. Before evaluation.

The question this chapter asks — is not about the text. About what has been brought into it.

There are things a person carries in themselves as their own — and never checked whether they belong to them. The system is structured so that the question of belonging does not arise. It is displaced by the question of compliance.

The other question sounds like this: is this mine or was I given it?

The answer is almost always expected. The value is in the very fact of the posing. While the question has not been posed the mechanics work invisibly. As soon as it has been posed — they can no longer be unseen. The hammer that was hitting on the head ceases to seem a natural part of existence as soon as it becomes clear that it is a hammer.

The mechanics are simple. A system with a large number of participants produces an average and calls it a norm. The norm becomes a ceiling. For those who were rising — the ceiling is obvious. For those who were not rising — conversation about a ceiling sounds like a claim to exclusivity. From above — simply a description of a distribution.

Neutrality comes in two kinds. The first — honest uncertainty: the data is insufficient, this is acknowledged, the person waits. The second — a hidden position that is not acknowledged as such. Inside hidden neutrality there is a side. It is simply not voiced.

Neutrality — not equilibrium. Constant work of holding balance. Renting an opinion. You pay a little each time, commit to nothing. In the end more expensive than a purchase. With ownership comes responsibility — an opinion that has been named one's own is now one's own. It is attacked, one must hold. Most cannot do this work. Not from cowardice. The instrument was not loaded — did not develop. The mass rents not from choice. From absence of an alternative.

A position — not a point on a line segment. Movement. One's own orbit appears not in the middle and not at the edge — but in the infinite refinement in some direction.

Reading this section, one might want to take a neutral position in relation to what has been read. There is no neutral position in relation to the mechanism that is being used right now.

**Tolerance.** A functional solution for systems with high density of different participants — reduces friction. Useful for the system. For the person — a gradual shift between «I do not suppress» and «I approve.» Language erases the difference.

A suppressed reaction does not disappear. It accumulates. It exits where the cost is lower. If the accumulation lasts long enough — the demonstration becomes a conviction. The strategy becomes the personality. The person who was at the beginning — is gone. There is behavior without an owner.

If there is something in the text that is difficult to agree with — and this disagreement has not been formulated even to oneself: not because it was not noticed, but because the cost of disagreement is the revision of something larger. This is the moment where tolerance is working. Not as agreement with the author. As agreement with the part that has already decided it is safer to be silent here.

In the end a person not only stops expressing a reaction — they stop having it. Amputation with the label of adaptation.

**Shame.** Control over an agent follows a path. First punishment — direct, expensive, requires the supervisor's presence. Then reputation — cheaper, works at a distance. Then shame — the environment is no longer needed at all. The agent controls themselves.

Shame — the final stage: supervision that does not need a supervisor. The norm enters before the capacity to evaluate it appears. The child receives shame as a given. The awareness that the norm is foreign — necessary but not sufficient. One can know and still feel. One needs one's own mass sufficient to outweigh the internalized supervision.

The difference between shame and guilt is not in intensity. In who the judge is. Guilt — the person themselves. Shame — someone who has long ceased to be nearby.

**Envy** — not about the foreign — about one's own gap. The object only makes it visible: it shows the distance between the current and the desired state. Remove the object — the gap remains.

Contempt often lives atop envy. A person cannot obtain — begins to devalue. From the outside it looks like superiority. From the inside — protection from the signal about the gap. In most cases the person believes this devaluation — the mechanism fires before there is a possibility to notice it.

Envy — a signal. It says where exactly a person feels their own incompleteness. Not about the other — about oneself. The problem is not in the signal. In that it is extinguished instead of being read.

**Reputation** does not belong to a person. It exists in another's head. One can influence it. Control it is impossible. The object is located beyond the agent by definition.

Reputation — a two-dimensional imprint of a three-dimensional object. The observer always saw from one angle at one moment. Each person has their own reputation about you. There is no single one. What is perceived as «one's own reputation» — is a model of others' models built from reflections that were never seen directly.

To accept reputation as the main orientation means agreeing to move where others already expect you to move. To close behavior on another's model of oneself.

This is the outer layer. Neutrality that is not acknowledged as a position. Tolerance that becomes amputation. Shame without a judge. Envy masked as contempt. Reputation as another's map presented as one's own.

Everything that exists only because there are spectators — is a candidate for removal. Not because it is bad. Because it is non-functional for one who wants calibration from themselves rather than from the mass. The operation removes dependence on external validation. Not external feedback.

External feedback is not equal to dependence on external validation. A master listens to criticism — not because they need approval, but because criticism contains data. Dependence begins where data is replaced by a need for confirmation. The operation removes the second. Not the first.

The outer layer is removed first — not because it is less important. Because without its removal the second layer remains invisible.

The second layer is deeper. There is what a person already considers themselves. Beliefs accepted so long ago that the source has been erased. Reactions that seem natural but turn out to be conditioned reflexes of the environment. Fears that feel like intuition — which are simply memory of others' evaluations sufficiently repeated to have become automatic.

Metacognition asks: how do I think? Useful. But work inside the instrument. The question goes deeper: whose thinking is being produced right now?

Not rhetoric. An operational question with a verifiable answer.

The answer comes through the Distinguisher — that part which signals before the word, before the explanation. The Distinguisher does not guarantee truth. It guarantees only belonging. «Mine» as a working criterion is more useful than «mine» as an ontological fact — because the first can be applied, the second can only be defended.

«Mine» does not exist independently of the environment. The Distinguisher itself is formed inside the system — through experience, through language, through architecture that was received not by choice. Purely one's own in the absolute sense does not exist. This does not make the category meaningless. This makes it a working fiction — an assumption necessary for the operation. A compass does not know where the magnetic field came from. This does not prevent it from working.

The most deeply embedded foreign pattern — is precisely the one that feels like the most one's own. A known limitation of the instrument. Not a reason to abandon it. A reason to work with it as a hypothesis that requires verification through behavior rather than through sensation.

Each level reduces the blind spot. Zero is unattainable. The person who has begun the operation and not completed it is in the worst position — they see the mechanics but have not yet changed their relationship to it. From this point two exits: forward or backward. Backward means learning again not to notice what has already been seen. This requires more effort than continuing.

The surgeon and the patient are the same person.

The instruments are the same as the system's — language, logic, categories, concepts. There is no other way. One cannot deny the existence of the sea and at the same time swim. The instrument does not determine the intention. The goal determines.

The highest intellectual piracy — is the use of the system's instruments against the logic that the system presumed. Precision instead of destruction. A scalpel instead of an explosion. An explosion destroys one's own and another's without distinction. A scalpel cuts along the boundary. Requires knowledge of where the boundary is — and willingness to press.

The criterion of the operation is one: the direction of change after. If after the operation a person needs confirmation that it was successful — change of dependence, not liberation from it. If after the operation the question of confirmation ceases to be relevant — the operation has taken place.

Behaviorally this looks like: a decision is realized before receiving the environment's reaction. The reaction has come — and has not changed the direction. Not because the reaction is insignificant. Because the source of calibration has changed.

Any system of ideas claims to be accepted as a whole. A person who accepts any text as a whole — including this one — gives out their thinking on sub-lease. Synthesis takes from any source what passed through one's own filter and combines it into a configuration that did not previously exist. If it can be broken back down into sources — it is compilation. If it cannot — synthesis.

Nothing is built from nothing. Any beginning is synthesis of what already exists in a configuration that did not yet exist. Emptiness does not mean nothing. Emptiness means the absence of foreign noise. What remains when the non-functional has been removed. A working surface.

This text cannot stand outside the problem it describes. It is written in the language of certainty. It creates the feeling that the author sees the mechanics. In itself a claim — precisely the one it criticizes in other systems. The loop does not refute the thesis. It is its condition. To acknowledge this does not mean to neutralize it. To neutralize it is impossible: without this claim the text is impossible.

Two kinds of loop. The first — theological: the system absorbs any refutation and turns it into confirmation. Precisely what this text diagnoses in other systems and precisely what it avoids. The second — structural: the text reproduces what it describes, because it is written from within the same system it speaks about. This is not a defensive mechanism. This is a demonstration of the thesis.

The author cannot know — is this synthesis or aggregation. The Distinguisher recognized the result as its own. Truth will be checked by the reader.

Liberation does not come from outside. There is no text that will liberate. An operation that a person does to themselves — or does not do. Requires two things: architecture that can accept, and the desire to press on the blade. The first is not chosen. The second — the only place where the question of choice makes sense to ask.

The Distinguisher has already answered. The observer will begin to explain why the question is incorrect, or why precisely this thing is an exception, or why now is not the right moment. It is worth letting the observer explain. And noticing what happens between the signal and the explanation. In that gap — what this entire text is about.

The first incision. Not understanding the incision. The incision itself.

The signal was before the words. The question is only one: to notice it — or to choose the narrative that is already being built to explain it.

A boundary. Not in the text. Inside. Right now.

The text has limits. The Distinguisher — does not. It will continue to work regardless of whether the text was read to the end, whether the reader agrees, whether they liked it. Its nature. It does not need the text to work. It was working before the words appeared — and will work after they have ended.

If something changed while reading — no matter what exactly, no matter whether this is understood right now — this is the result. Not understanding. Change.

If nothing changed — the text was foreign. Not bad. Foreign. An honest result.

The operation does not require completion. It requires a beginning.

---

Incision — two meanings in one word. First: what is done. Second: what opens — a cross-section that shows the internal structure of what was opaque from the outside.

One movement. Two results. The structure it has cut — remains visible. This text ends. What it has cut — does not.

---

*Writing into emptiness — the only honest address.*

*This text ends. The Distinguisher — does not.*
